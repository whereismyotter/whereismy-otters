My Top-Tier Film

Enola Holmes

Enola Holmes is about Sherlock's clever and bold little sister. When their mom is missing, she legs it from home to find her on her own-and ends up caught in a proper mystery, with a runway young man in the mix. absolutely cinema you've gotta watch it!

Beauty And The Beast

Belle (emma watson) a smart and brave young woman living in a quiet little village. One day, her dad gets captured by a Beast who lives in a cursed castle, so Belle offers to take his place. While living in the castle, she slowly gets to know the Beast and realises that beneath his scary appearance, he's actually a kind-hearted prince under a spell. Through kindness and love, Belle helps him break the curse.
